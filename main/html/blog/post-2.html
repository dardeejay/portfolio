Esports - a new kind of sports

Esports or electronic sports is a form of competition using video games. There are professional leagues and amateur leagues. It was also first included in the 2019 South East Asian Games held here in the Philippines and were featured at the 2018 Asian Games as a demonstration sport and will be included as medal games at the 2022 Asian Games that will be held in Hangzhou, China.


One of the biggest esports even is the Dota 2's The International, which features a staggering amount of money for its prize pool, this year's The International featured 40 million dollars in prize pool which 18 million dollars will go to the champions.


Esports is also one of the biggest market right now. With over 121 million people watching every year it is here to stay.
