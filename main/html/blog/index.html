Cryptocurrency

During these past few years, we have seen drastic increase in the popularity of Cryptocurrency. So what is it?


A Cryptocurrency is a type of digital currency that can be used to buy goods and services online. It uses blockchain technology, Blockchain is a decentralized system that handles and records transactions across numerous computers. Cryptocurrencies are not managed by any goverment or any entity unlike fiat money that is controlled by a certain central bank.


What caused the rise in popularity of Cryptocurrencies?


During this pandemic, the world has seen a massive increase on the usage of the internet. People rely more and more on online shoppings and contactless payment options and cryptocurrency is one of that option. Another factor in the increase in popularity of cryptocurrency is the NFT market and Play-to-earn games such as Axie Infinity and Cryptoblades.
